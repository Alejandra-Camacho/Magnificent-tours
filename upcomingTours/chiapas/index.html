Chiapas

Let's go to Chiapas!! Visit the beautiful Mayan Ruins of Palenque, Pueblo Magico of San Cristobal de las casas, Lacandona jungle, Bonampak ruins, Yaxchilan, La Venta Museum, The amazing Sumidero Canyon, The fantastic Mayan Villages and wonderful Agua Azul Waterfalls, Lakes of Montebello and El Chiflon Waterfalls.

7 days 6 nights trip!

DATES:
March 17th to march 23th.

INTINERARY:
Day 1: Fly from Guadalajara to Tuxtla Gutierrez
Full-day tour: combines visits to Sumidero Canyon and the Magic Town of Chiapa de Corzo
Boat ride on the Grijalva River, in the shadow of towering canyon walls.
Learn about the local flora and fauna from your guide.
Walk through the Magic Town of Chiapa de Corzo to learn about it's history.
Hotel in San Cristobal de las Casas

Day 2: Breakfast included. Amatenango del Valle is an indigenas community very well known for its pottery.
El Chiflon waterfalls where we will see the waterfall named brides bail which is 120 meters, and if you like an extreme adventure you can ride the zip line for an extra cost.
Montebello Lakes, The lakes of montebello which are conformed by 56 lakes of different sizes and tonalities of surprising colors that range from green, blue and turquoise, tonalities that are due to the refraction of the light.
San Cristóbal de las Casas, go back to our hotel!

Day 3: Breakfast included
Full-day trip to two Mayan communities, Zinacantan, and Chamula.
Enjoy the gorgeous scenery of the Chiapan mountains.
We will finalize by going back to the magic Town of San Cristóbal de las Casas to make a run in this beautiful city in which we will be able to admire the market, the catedral the church of santo domingo as well as a walk through the main tourist strips.

Day 4: Breakfast included
Full-day tour to Palenque and two gorgeous waterfalls.
Guided visit to Palenque, an UNESCO World Heritage Site.
Chance to look at Chiapas' scenic interior jungles.
Visit waterfalls of Agua Azul and Misol Há.
Back to hotel in the city of PALENQUE.

Day 5: Breakfast included
Day trip to visit part of the Lacandona Jungle, and our first stop will be at the archeological site of Bonampak, this Mayan city is very important because of its mural on the walls of the temples.
After visiting Bonampak, we'll go straight to another archeological site named Yaxchilan, it is majestic because of its location, history, and it's architecture. It is located in a peninsula by the river Usumacinta.
After our day trip we'll go to Lacanja camp where we'll spend the night in very nice cabins.
Dinner is included!

Day 6: Breakfast included.
After breakfast, we will spend part of the day with the indigenas people of Hach Winik which means "true people". We will learn about their great knowledge of these direct descendants of the mayans from Palenque, Bonampak and Yaxchilan.
Then we will go to into the jungle where we will be able to enjoy all the flora and fauna that this beautiful place has to offer.
The last part of our day trip, we will have a chance to go in the water at the amzing Mactuni-Ha waterfall.
Back to the city of PALENQUE where we will spend the night.

Day 7: Breakfast included
Check out from hotel, and then visit the museum of La Venta in the city of villahermosa.
In this museum we will be able to see some of the colosal Olmeca Heads, another interesting thing about this museum, it is part of the jungle where we might be able to see some raccoons, deer, and monkeys.
After that we'll be going to the airport to head back to Guadalajara.ssss
