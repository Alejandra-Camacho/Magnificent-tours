Whale Watching

Magnificent Tours is ready to go Whale watching and swimming with Sea Lions and Whale Sharks!! This adventure takes place in Baja California South. We go whale watching in the Magdalena bay going out of Puerto San Carlos. On the following days we have another fantastic experience by going swimming with sea lions and whale sharks! The south of the Baja, has a wide variety of beautiful places to see, and guess what? THIS IS YOUR CHANCE TO SEE THEM!

This is a 6 day, 5 night trip!

DATES:
February 27th to March 4th 2019.
Leave from Guadalajara Airport, February 27th 2019.

INTINERARY:
Day 1: (Breakfast included) Arrive to La Paz and transport to Puerto San Carlos, free day!
Day 2: (Breakfast included) Our first great adventure! Whale watching. Boat ride from Puerto San Carlos, during this boat trip we'll be able to see the island of the birds, sea lions, dolphins (high probability), whale watching, visit Magdalena Island, some time for lunch (lobster, shrimp, fish. Not included in the price), mangroves and a walk on the sand dunes, drinks are included on the boat! Back to hotel.
Day 3: (Breakfast included) Drive to La Paz, check in hotel, and free day. The hotel is right on the malecon. Day 4: (Breakfast included) Another Magnificent experience. Boat ride to go swimming with the sea lions, visit the island of Espíritu Santo, lunch there included on the cost, after that boat ride to swim with the gigantic whale shark, and then back to the hotel.
Day 5: (Breakfast included) A relaxing day! Visiting different beaches close to La Paz (this places will leave you with your mouth open, it's beautiful) . Some time for lunch and back to the hotel around 6 pm.
Day 6: (Breakfast included) Breakfast in hotel, some free time, and then back to Guadalajara!
