Tequila

Join our fabulous day trips to Pueblo Magico of Tequila! We visit the agave fields and La Alborada distillery, a family owned organic tequila distillery!

Previous Next

On this trip you'll learn about how to grow and take care of the agave plant, you will also learn the process of making tequila after the agave plant has been harvested, and after that you'll get to taste the premium brand of Tequila made by this distillery!
After that you will have some time to eat lunch, and continue with our tour through town, where you will learn about it's amazing history.
